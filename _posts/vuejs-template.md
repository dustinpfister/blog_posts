The [vue template option](https://vuejs.org/v2/api/#template) is one of the DOM options when making an instance of Vue in [vuejs](https://vuejs.org/). It is a way of defining a template that will be used in place of any content that might be in the mount point set with the [vue el](/2019/05/06/vuejs-el/) option.
